The problem

To prove a payroll total…
you expose every salary?

Today, verifying a company's total payroll to an auditor or investor means revealing everyone's individual pay. The most sensitive number in the company, laid bare — just to prove a sum.

The primitive

What if the contract could compute on ciphertext?

Fully Homomorphic Encryption lets a smart contract add, multiply and compare numbers while they stay encrypted. Zama FHEVM brings that to the EVM.

FHE.fromExternal
Encrypted inputs with a zero-knowledge proof — the contract never sees plaintext.
FHE.add / mul / div
Homomorphic arithmetic: sum salaries, compute a bonus — all on ciphertext.
FHE.lt + select
Encrypted comparison & conditional — an overflow guard built from FHE.
makePubliclyDecryptable
Reveal ONLY the total — salaries stay encrypted. The audit primitive.
